Mitski

Mitski is a Japanese-American singer, songwriter, and musician, born on September 27, 1990, in Kyoto, Japan, and raised in various places around the world, such as Turkey, China, and the Democratic Republic of Congo. She is known for her emotionally intense music and confessional personal lyrics that address themes such as love, loneliness, anxiety, and cultural identity.

Mitski released her first album, "Lush", in 2012 while studying at Purchase College in New York. Her second album, "Retired from Sad, New Career in Business", released in 2013, gained attention in the indie rock scene and attracted a growing fan base.

Over the years, Mitski has become known for her captivating live performances, which often feature her intense stage presence and emotionally charged vocals. With a devoted fanbase and a growing reputation as one of indie rock's most exciting new voices, Mitski is poised to continue making an impact on the music world for years to come.
